Feature Overview

Built to Help You Actually Pass

Not just another question bank. A complete study system with lessons, community support, and intelligent tracking.

The Problem

"I'm just memorizing answers, not learning"

Most study tools throw questions at you without explaining the concepts. You pass the test but don't understand the material—and that's not great for actually using your radio.

Question drills without context
No explanation of the "why"
Pattern memorization doesn't stick
The Solution

Learning Library

Real lessons organized by topic—not just question drills.

  • Educational content with table of contents
  • External resources for deeper learning
  • Related questions linked to each topic
  • Progress tracking per topic
The Problem

"I don't know what I don't know"

You're spending hours studying, but are you focusing on the right things? Most people waste 60% of their study time on questions they already know.

No visibility into weak areas
Re-studying what you've already mastered
No way to know if you're ready
The Solution

Weak Question Tracking

We identify your problem areas so you don't waste time.

  • Tracks every question you miss
  • Streak-based drills to master weak areas
  • Readiness scoring shows when you're prepared
  • Dashboard highlights areas needing work
The Problem

"I'm stuck and have no one to ask"

You hit a confusing topic and the explanation doesn't help. Who do you ask? Searching online gives conflicting answers. You need real help from real hams.

Studying in isolation
No one to ask when you're stuck
Missing the ham radio community
The Solution

Community Forum

Active Discourse community of hams helping hams.

  • Ask questions, get real answers
  • Study tips from people who've passed
  • Connect with the ham community early
  • Linked to your Open Ham Prep account
Visit forum.openhamprep.com
Study Modes

Multiple Ways to Practice

Choose the study mode that fits your learning style

Practice Tests

Full-length exams that mirror the real test format. Includes optional timer for exam simulation.

  • 35 or 50 questions per test
  • Immediate scoring
  • Detailed review after

Random Practice

Quick question drills in random order. Build confidence without pattern memorization.

  • Instant feedback
  • Streak tracking
  • Study at your pace

Study by Topics

Focus on specific subelements. Great for targeting weak areas or reviewing specific subjects.

  • Organized by subelement
  • Topic overviews
  • Progress per topic

Weak Questions

Review questions you've missed. Streak mode requires 3 correct in a row to clear.

  • Auto-identified weak areas
  • Streak-based mastery
  • Visual progress tracking

Glossary Flashcards

Master ham radio terminology with spaced-repetition flashcards. Study term→definition or reverse.

  • 100+ essential terms
  • Two-way flashcards
  • Mastery tracking

Bookmarks & Notes

Save questions for later review. Add personal notes to remember key insights.

  • Save any question
  • Personal notes
  • Separate review mode
