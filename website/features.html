Open Ham Prep is packed with features designed to help you study efficiently, track your progress, and pass the FCC amateur radio license exam on your first try.

Practice questions in random order to build well-rounded knowledge.

  • Track your streak

Practice Tests

  • Targeted learning
  • Topic overviews

  • Set target exam

  • Track mastery
